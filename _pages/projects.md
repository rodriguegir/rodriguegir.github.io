## Codes

**Monte Carlo Pricer for Multi-Asset Structured Products** (2024)  
*Python: Correlated GBM, Cholesky decomposition, zero-coupon curve bootstrapping*  
Prices a 7-year principal-protected note linked to a basket of 9 US equities. Implements full Greeks via bump-and-revalue (Delta, Vega, Rho, correlation risk) and bootstraps a zero-coupon curve from market rates.  
[[GitHub](https://github.com/rodriguegir/Personal-works/tree/230a4f6e6c021aa05160c5b2f66abd756fc7129a/monte_carlo_pricing)]

**Demographic Ageing and Public Debt: A Cointegration Analysis** (2025)  
*Python: Engle-Granger and Mean Group estimators, Eurostat & IMF WEO data*  
Estimates the long-run relationship between demographic ageing and public debt dynamics across 20 European countries.  
[[GitHub](https://github.com/rodriguegir/Personal-works/tree/230a4f6e6c021aa05160c5b2f66abd756fc7129a/cointegration_analysis)]
